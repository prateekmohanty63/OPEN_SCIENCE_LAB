1. EFFICIENCY

With significant growth, usage, and awareness in handling the internet regardless of distance, time, and age factors. The proposal brings an exciting and promising opportunity for society to access Open Science Laboratories (OSLs) to conduct real experiments at a physical location via the internet and fetch real-time results for their benefit

OSL will be a fully aimed to build in fully automated mode, the user sample will be sent to the location, on receiving it the user will be notified with a tracking ID and the user can customize the parameters regarding the process they intend to. The lab provides the flexibility to set the quality of the reagents, thermal setting, reaction time, and so on

OPEN SCIENCE LAB

01

Access: Make the scientific research equipment available to researchers across all universities and research institutes in India – not just Tier 1 institutes with big budgets and large teams.

02

Collaboration: make it possible for remote researchers to collaborate and run shared experiments. Make it easier for collaborators to enhance experimental setups suggested by other researchers and make rapid progress.

03

Replication: Make it easier for researchers to replicate experimental setup and results reported by other researchers. This can help to alleviate the replication crisis.

04

Citizen science: Make it easier for independent researchers to run custom experiments based on their hypotheses.
